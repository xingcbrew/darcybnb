Guest Rooms

Zen Queen

This is a spacious room with an ensuite bathroom and work/dining table. The room has a variety of calming plants.

Cozy Double

The Double Room is a cozy space that will fit one or two guests. The room has its own den with a dining/work table and an ensuite bathroom.

Cozy Queen

Tucked away at the end of the house, this south-facing room offers guests privacy and a quiet night's rest. This room shares a bathroom with the guest staying in the Solo room.

Solo

This is the perfect room for a solo traveller or third person in a group. The room is cozy and has a work desk. This room shares a bathroom with the Cozy Queen.

Shared Spaces
